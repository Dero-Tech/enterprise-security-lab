# NorthStar Health Systems (NHS)

## Company Overview

NorthStar Health Systems is a simulated healthcare organization designed to demonstrate enterprise Identity and Access Management (IAM), cloud security, and security operations practices.

The organization manages sensitive healthcare information including Protected Health Information (PHI), employee records, financial data, and operational systems.

## Business Environment

Industry:
Healthcare

Organization Size:
5,000 employees

Locations:
- 3 Hospitals
- 10 Outpatient Clinics
- Remote workforce

Security Objective:

Build a secure identity architecture that protects patient information while allowing authorized employees to efficiently access the systems required for their roles.

---

# Identity Challenges

The organization must solve:

1. How do employees receive access when hired?
2. How is access removed when employees leave?
3. How do we prevent excessive permissions?
4. How do we monitor suspicious authentication?
5. How do we prove compliance during audits?

---

# Security Principles

NorthStar follows:

- Least privilege access
- Zero Trust security
- Multi-factor authentication
- Role-Based Access Control
- Continuous monitoring
- Identity governance
